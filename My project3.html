Hello, dear, everybody! My name is Julia. Nice to meet you on my CV webpage. You are placed on my first project of myself. That’s why take your time to ask me questions and invite me on an interview.

At least I decided to learn the Frontend Development: first knowledges I got from a several Coursera’s Courses, at once started and continue to go to IT meetups, where found interesting people with whom I could be on one wave.
On my webpage you will find information about me, you will have a possibility to open my CV and download it, timeline of my work experience which surely influence to my personality and my skills. For certain in section “Skills” I will leave the skills which is most usable for Frontend for sure. In my footer I have a possibility to see my Github and Linkedin page. You are welcome!
